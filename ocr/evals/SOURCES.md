## Receipt image

File: `receipt_cord.jpg`

Source: CORD v2 dataset, `split=test`, `image_id=6`.

> Seunghyun Park, Seung Shin, Bado Lee, Junyeop Lee, Jaeheung Surh, Hoon Cho,
> Chuanming Jia (2019). CORD: A Consolidated Receipt Dataset for
> Post-OCR Parsing. Workshop on Document Intelligence at NeurIPS 2019.

Dataset repository: https://huggingface.co/datasets/naver-clova-ix/cord-v2

License: **CC BY 4.0** — https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

The card number visible in the receipt is pre-masked in the source dataset
(`xx7730`). No additional PII scrubbing was required.
